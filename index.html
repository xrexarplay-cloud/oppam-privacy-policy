1. Introduction

ExarPlay ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Oppam Game handles information when you use our mobile game on Android or iOS devices.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Oppam Game currently does not collect any personal or sensitive information.

  • 🚫 No personal information (name, email, phone number, or address)
  • 🚫 No financial or payment information
  • 🚫 No location tracking
  • 🚫 No analytics or advertising trackers

The game does not require account registration or login.

3. Information Stored Locally

The game may store limited gameplay data locally on your device.

  • 🎮 Gameplay progress
  • 🏆 Scores or achievements
  • ⚙️ Player settings such as sound or control preferences

This information never leaves your device and will be deleted if you uninstall the game.

4. Data Security

Because Oppam Game does not collect or transmit personal data to external servers, the risk of external data breaches is minimized.

All gameplay data remains stored locally on the player's device.

5. Children's Privacy

Oppam Game does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will address the issue promptly.

6. Third-Party Platforms

Oppam Game may be distributed through digital stores such as the Google Play Store and the Apple App Store.

These platforms may collect technical information according to their own privacy policies.

7. Your Rights

Because Oppam Game does not collect personal data:

  • No personal data is stored on our servers
  • You may remove all locally stored game data by uninstalling the game
